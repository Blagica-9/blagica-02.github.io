Visiting places in our city

Visiting places in our city is my favorite hobby that I do every week. My friends and I visit some places near our city, such as Markukule, the artificial Prilep Lake, and a number of monasteries that are located in nature, on foot or by bicycle. This hobby is resting my brain from a busy week. And the benefit is not only the physical activity that is healthy, but also the magic of nature and the knowledge of the history of our city that we will find out.

My favourite series

My favorite part of my free time is watching this wonderful work. In a difficult period when we needed entertainment the most, I started watching this series. And so far I have seen it about 6 times.
